About Us

BuildSoc (Building Smarter Societies) is a Policy and Urban Tech Studio that bridges governance, planning, and data. We work at the intersection of urban policy, spatial technology, and digital governance — enabling cities to become smarter, more inclusive, and evidence-driven.

Founded by young urban planners and technologists, BuildSoc rethinks how cities are shaped — not just through plans, but through data, design, and decision systems.

Our Mission

To create data-driven, technology-enabled urban systems that make cities more equitable, sustainable, and responsive.

Our Vision

To be India’s most forward-thinking Urban Policy & Technology Think Tank, transforming research and data into real change for cities and citizens.

Founded by Bharat Oraon and Manish Yalala — urban planners driven by innovation, governance, and data.
